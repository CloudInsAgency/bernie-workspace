# ZeroSugar Club — Marketing & Influencer Campaign — Handoff

## Purpose
ZeroSugar Club is an iOS health app that helps users quit sugar through a 30-day challenge, accountability clubs, meal plans, and workouts. This project encompasses the complete marketing strategy, including SEO content creation, influencer marketing campaign setup (specifically targeting Collabstr micro-influencers), and TikTok creator briefs. The goal is to drive app downloads through authentic social proof and performance-based partnerships on a $300 budget, with initial focus on photo carousel-based TikTok creators who don't speak English or prefer non-verbal content.

## Key Decisions

**Marketing Strategy:**
- **Micro-influencers over macro**: 10K-50K follower range, higher engagement (5-10%), more affordable, better authenticity
- **Flat fee model**: $150 per influencer, no commission tracking (due to Apple limitations and budget constraints)
- **Photo carousel format**: Specifically targeting TikTok creators who do visual/carousel content with trending sounds (no talking required)
- **5-day turnaround**: Fast content creation timeline rather than 30-day challenge documentation (practical for first test)
- **Performance tracking via unique links**: zerosugarclub.com/[creatorname] using Bitly or similar
- **No exaggerated claims**: Removed "15,000+ users" and "1,200+ clubs" references to avoid verification issues

**Content Decisions:**
- **SEO-focused blog article**: Targets all primary keywords (sugar free app, quit sugar, no sugar diet, sugar tracker, etc.)
- **Science-backed positioning**: Emphasizes peer-reviewed research (ASTD 95% accountability stat, Obesity journal social support data, AHA guidelines)
- **Avoid displacement**: Article doesn't recreate website structure; provides complementary educational content
- **Copyright compliance**: Max 15 words per quote, one quote per source, heavy paraphrasing

**Apple App Store Limitations:**
- **No custom affiliate tracking**: Apple only offers 2.5% commission through their program, doesn't allow custom per-influencer codes
- **Workaround options documented**: Honor system with referral code entry in app, web-based purchase with redemption codes, or flat fee only
- **Current approach**: Flat fee + manual tracking via unique landing page URLs

- Last slide = CTA ("Link in bio", "Download now")
- Trending sounds matter more than perfect production quality
